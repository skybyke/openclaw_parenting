## Key Topics

### Spring Break Discipline Strategies
Scout suggested four approaches:
1. **Build on "making things"** — 30 min daily project time before screens unlock (art, cooking, safe metalworking). Discipline is showing up at the same time, not the content.
2. **One small routine anchor** — 10-15 min reading or journaling before lunch. Low stakes, maintains the muscle.
3. **Gamify it lightly** — Break-long challenge with tangible reward. "Hit 7 of 9 days = pick Sunday dinner." Plays to Archer's motivators (steak, choice).
4. **Body doubling** — Parallel focus time with a parent. "I'm doing X for 20 min, want to do your thing next to me?"

Core principle: keep the *rhythm* without the *content* of school. He's practicing the initiation skill, not academics.

Candy liked the ideas.
