## Accommodation Plan

All accommodations target **Executive Functioning** and are implemented in **General Education Classes** by the **General Education Teacher**.

### Fidget Use
Archer will be permitted to utilize a fidget (provided by school or home) to aide in regulation and focus. Must use this tool appropriately (quietly, keeping it in his hand/on his desk).

### Preferential Seating
Archer will be provided with a seating assignment towards the front and/or middle of the classroom in order to limit distractions and allow for quick redirections from the teacher.

### Copy of Teacher Notes
Archer will be provided with either guided notes or a copy of class notes when available to assist in reducing handwriting when necessary. Teacher can request notes and/or key vocabulary be copied by hand by Archer to assist with retention of information.

### Repeating Directions
When full-group instructions are given for assignments with multiple steps, Archer will be given the directions again to facilitate his executive functioning and allow for an opportunity to ask clarifying questions.

### Speech-to-Text
Archer can utilize speech-to-text when doing longer (more than a paragraph) writing assignments as permitted by the teacher on certain assignments.

### Organizational Support
Archer's teachers will check in with him at least weekly to address any missing work and support his organizational abilities (e.g., having Archer go through his folders to find missing work).

### Backpack
To support his organization, Archer will be permitted to carry his backpack during the school day.
